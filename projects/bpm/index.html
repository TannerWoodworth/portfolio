ABOUT US

Foundation

BluePaper, originating from the term Blueprint, is a small web design agency based in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. The company was founded by two individuals with backgrounds emcompassing mobile development, designing mobile applications, and product management and design. The two have had a wide variety of clientele ranging from small ad agencies to corporate banks.

Ethos

Bluepaper Media offers user-centric design paired with innovative technologies to help get audiences engaged with their content. We strive to make every pixel meaningful and push the limits of today's web standards. We love what we do and enjoy the process.

OUR PROCESS

  • Research

    "A goal without a plan is just a wish." — Antoine de Saint-Exupéry


    We couldn't have said it better ourselves. Any new project we begin working on is met with careful planning and extensive research. This helps us to understand the goals and needs of the project and what we need to do to aid the user in achieving their overall goal.

  • Markup & Design

    Once we have pinpointed the overall goal of a project, it is then time to design the easiest path for the user to follow to meet said aforementioned goal. By applying our research to our knowledge of cognitive psychology, we shape that path.

  • Code

    We then move on to build the project. At this stage we bring your vision to life. We work to make every keystroke as meaningful as we possibly can using the latest technologies that will still be usable for years to come.

  • Testing

    A working build is never enough. No matter how polished a project is, it can always be improved. We measure everything and work to optimize every part of the project to ensure we are getting the very most that we can as efficiently as we can.

  • Shipping & Delivery

    It is then time to move the project from production into the world. It's a lot like watching your child go to college, and you bet we get emotional.
