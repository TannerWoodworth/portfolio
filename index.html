About

About

My name is Tanner Woodworth and I am a front-end web developer based in Pittsburgh, PA. I am currently a student at Pittsburgh Technical Institute working on an Associate in Science Degree in Multimedia Technologies with a concentration in Web Design & Development.

I have been working with websites for a little over three years, and freelancing for two years with clients ranging from ad specialty agencies to assisted living homes.

Always Learning

The web is forever changing, and it is never going to stop. So why should I?

Aside from web, I love to dabble in the wonderful world of iOS development and write the occasional Python application.

Ethos

I believe that a design can never be too simple and designs can always be improved, no matter what.

Functionality over aesthetics, always.
