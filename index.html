About

About

My name is Tanner Woodworth and I am a front-end web developer based in Pittsburgh, PA.

I have attended school at Pittsburgh Technical College where I received my Associate in Science Degree in Multimedia Technologies with a concentration in Web Design and Development. While attending school at PTC, I had received numerous awards for academic acheivements as well as a Best in Show award for the Spring 2016 Portfolio show, and a 1st place Graphic Design Scholarship award.

Over the years I have had the opprotunity to work with a multitude of different clients. Aside from web work, I have also assisted numerous clients with video and photography projects.

Always Learning

The web is forever changing, and it is never going to stop. So why should I?

I try and squeeze as much learning into a day as I can, whether that means working through some online courses in my spare time, listening to a audiobook on my way to get groceries, or a podcast while clearning.

I do my best to select hobbies analogous to my career, such as podcasting, photography, and even a little game development.

Ethos

I have a strong desire to build a semantic and accessible web.

Design isn't art and functionality should be placed over aesthetics, always.

I believe it is best to avoid using trends in projects unless the project has a short life span such as an event site or poster.
